Project Overview

Small-scale fishermen often rely on manual methods such as notebooks, phone calls, and WhatsApp messages to manage customer orders and seafood availability. These methods may lead to forgotten orders, inaccurate sales records, and difficulty in updating customers about product availability.

FishMan aims to solve this problem by providing a centralized web-based platform where fishermen can manage products, track orders, view sales reports, and check weather and tide information before going out to sea.

Key Features

🛒

Order Management

Allows fishermen to view, confirm, complete, or reject customer orders in a more organized way.

🐟

Product Availability

Enables fishermen to add, update, and manage seafood availability based on daily catch.

🌦️

Weather Forecast

Provides weather information such as wind conditions to support safer fishing decisions.

🌊

Tide Information

Displays tide and sea water level information to help fishermen plan fishing activities.

📊

Sales Report

Generates daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly sales summaries for better record keeping.

📱

Customer Access

Customers can place orders without creating an account, making the system easier to use.
